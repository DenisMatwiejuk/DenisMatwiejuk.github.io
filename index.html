My Work

Lokir's labyrinth is a Metroidvania type project done during my time at university. The game was completed on the 10th of may 2018 and submitted as my final year project. The game follows Lokir who is a adventurer that was heading towards a castle in atop a mountain when he falls into a labyrinth from which he must find a way out.

The game was made using unity engine and can be downloaded here

Unnamed space shooter game is a attempt at a bullet hell game that was more wave based instead of having the player continuously moving forward. The game follows the unnamed pilot as they are the last of their fleet to live and must now find a way of defeating the enemy fleet alone.

The game was made using the Monogame engine and can be downloaded here
